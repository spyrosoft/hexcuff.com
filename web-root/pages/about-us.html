The Invention Of The Hex Cuff

One day a young man decided he wanted an ear cuff.

He typed "buy ear cuffs" into Google image search.

Digging, digging, and digging some more through the results, he became increasingly dissatisfied.

There were endless beautiful ear cuffs, but none which would work for him.

He considered what each of the ear cuffs he had seen had in common.

It occurred to him that hex nuts are already the correct shape.

If he removed one of the sides, the Hex Cuff should do well at attaching to his ear on its own.

He ventured out on a quest to the Hardware Store.

He picked out a few hex nuts to test his hypothesis.

He successfully battled and tamed his first Hex Cuff.

It worked better than he had dreamed.

He ventured out again, this time far and wide, in search of new types of hex nuts.

Thus, Hex Cuffs were born unto the world.
